Intuisity Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026

Intuisity helps users explore intuition, awareness, mindfulness, self-discovery, astrology insights, remote viewing practice, and personal growth through daily challenges.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide when you create an account, use the app, or invite a friend, including your name, email address, optional phone number, password, birthdate, optional birth time, birthplace, current city, state, country, language preference, friend contact information, challenge answers, scores, journal responses, module feedback, and admin/reporting activity data.

Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we may receive your Google account name, email address, and profile information that Google provides for authentication. We use this information to create or access your Intuisity account.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To save your daily challenge results, astrology details, responses, and progress.
  • To provide friend challenges and email invitations.
  • To improve Intuisity by reviewing module usage, ratings, comments, and engagement reports.
  • To provide customer support and security.

Friend Invites

If you invite a friend, you may provide their name, email address, and optional phone number. We use that information to send or prepare the challenge invitation and to show saved friend contacts in your account.

Service Providers

We may use trusted service providers to host, store, authenticate, and send messages for Intuisity, including services such as Vercel, Supabase, Resend, Google, and similar providers. These providers process information only as needed to support the app.

Cookies and Local Storage

Intuisity may use browser storage to remember login details, profile information, admin settings, challenge progress, and local analytics. You can clear browser storage in your browser settings, but doing so may remove saved progress on that device.

Health, Spiritual, and Entertainment Notice

Intuisity is for personal growth, reflection, entertainment, and self-discovery. It does not provide medical, mental health, legal, financial, or professional advice.

Children

Intuisity is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us. You may also stop using Intuisity or clear your browser storage at any time.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions can be sent to info@intuisity.com.
